## Outreach Strategy

### Priority 1 (High DA, High Relevance)
- TechCrunch, The Verge, Mashable, Gizmodo, CNET
- Approach: Expert commentary, news tips

### Priority 2 (Medium DA, High Relevance)
- Streaming-specific blogs, country-specific tech blogs
- Approach: Guest posts, expert quotes

### Priority 3 (Medium DA, Medium Relevance)
- Forums, communities, podcasts
- Approach: Helpful participation, guest appearances

### Priority 4 (Lower DA, High Relevance)
- Niche directories, local directories
- Approach: Directory listings

### Monthly Targets
- **Month 1:** 20 outreach emails to Priority 1 sites
- **Month 2:** 30 outreach emails to Priority 2 sites
- **Month 3:** 20 outreach emails to Priority 3 sites

- **Month 5:** 40 outreach emails to all priorities
- **Month 6:** 50 outreach emails to all priorities

### Success Metrics
- Target: 20% response rate
- Target: 10% conversion to backlink
- Goal: 100+ backlinks in 6 months
