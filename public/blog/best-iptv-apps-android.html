What to Look for in an IPTV App

Before diving into our reviews, here are the key features to consider:

  • Playlist Support: M3U, M3U8, Xtream Codes API
  • EPG Support: Electronic Program Guide for schedules
  • Video Quality: SD, HD, Full HD, 4K support
  • User Interface: Easy to navigate and use
  • Additional Features: Favorites, catch-up, recording, multi-screen
  • Performance: Fast loading, minimal buffering
  • Compatibility: Works with your Android version
