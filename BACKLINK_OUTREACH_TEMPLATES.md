## Email Outreach Best Practices

### Before Sending:
1. **Personalize Every Email:** Never send generic templates without customization
2. **Research the Recipient:** Know their content, audience, and interests
3. **Find the Right Contact:** Look for editors, bloggers, webmasters, or business owners
4. **Check Their Guidelines:** Many sites have guest post or link submission guidelines

### Subject Line Tips:
- Keep it under 50 characters
- Be specific and relevant
- Avoid spammy words
- Personalize with their name or website name
- Use numbers when appropriate

### Email Content Tips:
- Keep it concise (under 200 words if possible)
- Focus on value for them, not just what you want
- Be genuine and authentic
- Include a clear call-to-action
- Proofread for errors
- Include your contact information

### Follow-Up Strategy:
- Send first follow-up after 3-5 days
- Send second follow-up after 7-10 days
- Maximum 2-3 follow-ups
- Change subject line for follow-ups
- Add new value in each follow-up

### Tracking:
- Use email tracking to know when emails are opened
- Track response rates
- A/B test different subject lines
- Keep a spreadsheet of outreach efforts

### Response Handling:
- Respond promptly to positive responses
- Be flexible with their requirements
- Deliver high-quality content on time
- Build relationships for future opportunities
- Thank them even if they decline
